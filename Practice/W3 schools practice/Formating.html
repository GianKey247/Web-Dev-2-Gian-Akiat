There are unique ways to higlight or make your text pop

B tag is to make the text bold
Strong tag is to make the text look similar to bold
i tag is to make the text italic
em tag is to make the text look similar to italic
mark tag is to make the text highlighted
small tag is to make the text small
del tag is to make the text have a horizontal cut
ins tag is to make the text have a underline
Text sub tag is to make the text subscripted
Text sup tag is to make the text supscripted
